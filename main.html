Glow Sword Battle Seattle 2016

Dec 17th, 8pm

Join us for a massive glowing sword battle in Seattle. Pick a side, good or evil and use a glowing sword to fight in the ultimate nocturnal showdown. Appropriate for all ages.

RESERVE SWORD HERE: https://www.glowbattletour.com/collections/seattle

For every $10 you contribute ($7.50 early bird) we will reserve one glow sword for you. Each glow sword changes up to 6 colors, is about 3' long, and you will be able to pick them up at the event, or have them shipped to your house. We will start to gather at 8:00, split into two sides a little after 9:00, and the battle will begin. Glow on!
